# Financial Red Flags & Earnings Quality

Based on Thornton O'Glove's "Quality of Earnings" framework. Use this to identify warning signs that reported earnings may not reflect true business performance.

---

## The Core Principle

**Not all earnings are created equal.** Companies can manipulate reported earnings through accounting choices, timing, and accruals. Cash flow from operations is the most reliable metric because cash is hard to fake.

**The Golden Rule**: If reported earnings aren't translating to operating cash flow, investigate immediately.

---

## Critical Red Flags (Investigate Immediately)

### 1. Accounts Receivable Growing Faster Than Revenue

**What it means**: Company may be:
- Recognizing revenue before it's earned ("stuffing the channel")
- Having collection problems
- Offering extended payment terms to make sales

**How to check**:
- Calculate A/R growth rate vs. revenue growth rate
- Look at Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) trend
- Compare to industry peers
